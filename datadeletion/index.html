Data Deletion Request

Last updated: 2025

Illusion Interactive (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and gives you control over your personal data. This page explains how you can request the deletion of data associated with your use of our mobile applications.

What Data Can Be Deleted

  • Gameplay progress stored on our servers (if applicable)
  • User identifiers associated with analytics data
  • Crash reports and diagnostics linked to your user ID
  • Any support-related data you have shared with us

What Data Cannot Be Deleted

  • Purchase and transaction records handled by Google Play or Apple App Store
  • Data that must be retained for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please send an email to:
support@illusioninteractive.games

Include the following details in your email:

  • User ID (available in the app’s Settings page)
  • Name of the app
  • Platform (Android or iOS)

Processing Time

We will review and process data deletion requests within 30 days of receiving a valid request.

Third-Party Services

Our apps use third-party services such as Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics. Upon processing your request, we will remove or anonymize data under our control. Data processed by third-party services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Account-Free Usage

Our apps do not require users to create accounts. Data deletion is handled using the in-app User ID to identify and process requests.

Contact

If you have questions about this data deletion process, please contact us at:
support@illusioninteractive.games
