Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

Illusion Interactive ("we", "our", or "us") develops and publishes mobile games and applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our apps.

Information We Collect

1. Device & Technical Information

  • Device type, operating system, and version
  • Unique device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • IP address (used for analytics, fraud prevention, and regional features)

2. Usage & Analytics Data

  • Gameplay events and app interactions
  • Session duration and feature usage
  • In-app behavior and navigation patterns

3. Crash & Performance Data

  • Crash logs and error reports
  • Performance diagnostics and stability metrics

Advertising

Our apps may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use information such as advertising identifiers, device information, and approximate location (derived from IP address) to serve ads, measure performance, and prevent fraud.

Learn more about how Google uses data from partner apps: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our apps. Firebase Analytics may collect usage events, device information, and performance data to help us improve gameplay, fix bugs, and optimize app performance.

In-App Purchases

Our apps may offer in-app purchases. All purchases are processed securely by the respective platform providers:

  • Google Play Store (Android)
  • Apple App Store (iOS)

We do not collect or store payment details such as credit card numbers. All payment information is handled directly by the platform providers.

How We Use Information

  • Operate and maintain our apps
  • Improve gameplay, features, and user experience
  • Analyze usage, performance, and stability
  • Serve and measure advertisements
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal and platform requirements

Children’s Privacy

Our apps are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only with trusted third-party service providers such as:

  • Advertising partners (Google AdMob)
  • Analytics providers (Firebase)
  • Platform providers (Google Play, Apple App Store)

Your Choices

Depending on your device and region, you may reset or limit advertising identifiers, opt out of personalized ads, and manage app permissions through your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
